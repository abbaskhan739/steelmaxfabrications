Our Capabilities

1. Design

We provide front-end design services as part of our full-service custom metal fabrication offering. So if you know basically what you need, but aren’t sure how to get there, we can develop your concept into a properly certified specification. Just give us whatever you have — “stick man” drawings on the backs of paper napkins — and we’ll work it up

2. Plate / Angle Rolling and Bending

Most fabrication works are incomplete without a bend and roll ,thus we are equipped with these essential facilities.

3. Punching

Dealing with creative minds and fascinating ideas a punching machinery also comes to be very handy now a days.

4. Cutting

We provide metal slotting and cutting services making use of latest laser cutting tools and cnc machines.

5. Finishing / Cladding

Be it your mainstream powder coating and epoxy finishes or your wood And aluminium cladding ,you don’t have to worry about anything while working with us.
