Industrial Sheds/ Trusses


Industrial sheds are used to store raw materials or for product manufacturing. These industrial sheds are used for warehouse, factories, godowns, workshops, storage plants etc.

Sports Turf

Sports turf is a surface of synthetic fibers made to look like natural grass. It is most often used in arenas for sports that were originally or are normally played on grass. However, it is now being used on residential lawns and commercial applications as well.

Pergolas/ Cabana/ Gazebo

A Pergola/ Cabana/ Gazebo is an outdoor garden feature forming a shaded walkway, passageway, or sitting area of vertical posts or pillars that usually support cross-beams and a sturdy open lattice, often upon which woody vines are trained.

Tensile Fabric Canopies/ Structures

Tensile fabric structures are based on two basic building blocks of tensile structures. The beauty of a tensile fabric structure is the ability to design and create them around your specific requirements.

Spiral Staircases

Spiral staircases are a type of curved stairs that comprises of individual steps and are connected to a center column. Spiral staircases save valuable square meters because they occupy a much smaller area than a conventional staircase.

Rolling Shutters

Rolling shutter is a method of image capture in which a still picture (in a still camera) or each frame of a video (in a video camera) is captured not by taking a snapshot of the entire scene at a single instant in time but rather by scanning across the scene rapidly, either vertically or horizontally.

Bunker Beds

A piece of furniture consisting of two beds, one above the other, that form a unit. A bunk bed is a type of bed in which one bed frame is stacked on top of another, allowing two or more beds to occupy the floor space usually required by just one. They are commonly seen on ships, in the military, and in hostels, dormitories, summer camps etc.

Aluminium Fencing

An aluminum fence is a fence constructed primarily out of the element aluminium. Due to the metal's low density and ability to resist corrosion, it has become a popular choice as lightweight, durable fence and railing structure. Aluminum fencing is used for both commercial and residential use.

Wood Fencing

A Wooden Fence is defined as a barrier used for protection or to create a boundary. It can be used in Cafes and Garden to give a beautiful boundaries and decoration to the surrounding.

Chainlink Fencing

A chain-link fence is a type of woven fence usually made from galvanized or LLDPE-coated steel wire. The wires run vertically and are bent into a zig-zag pattern so that each "zig" hooks with the wire immediately on one side and each "zag" with the wire immediately on the other.

Wrought Iron Fencing

Wrought iron is an iron alloy with a very low carbon content in contrast to that of cast iron. Fencing is made using Wrought Iron to give a strong boundary to our residential and commercial properties.

Vinyl Fencing

A synthetic fence, plastic fence or vinyl or PVC fence is a fence made using synthetic plastics, such as vinyl, polypropylene, nylon, polythene ASA, or from various recycled plastics. Composites of two or more plastics can also be used to increase strength and UV stability of a fence.

Barbwired Fencing

Barbed wire, also known as barb wire, occasionally corrupted as bobbed wire or bob wire, is a type of steel fencing wire constructed with sharp edges or points arranged at intervals along the strands. It is used to construct inexpensive fences and is used atop walls surrounding secured property.

Telescopic Gates
