About SteelMax Fabrications

Who Are We?

We are Metal Fabrication company based in Mumbai.

We have over 20 years of experience in this industry having done almost all types of metal fabrication jobs.

We apply every bit of this experience into our projects for complete client satisfaction taking into consideration all his requirements while keeping a check on the affordability and timely completion of the work.

We take care of the project right from material procurement till complete delivery and installation.

We pride ourselves in accountability, accessibility and building strong relationships with our customers. Understanding and meeting the needs of customers who are adapting to a constantly changing business environment is what makes us reliable and easy to work with.
We work diligently to grow and update our facilities to meet your needs while never sacrificing honesty and integrity .

  • Competitive Rates

  • Highest Quality Standard of Service
